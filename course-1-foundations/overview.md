# Course 1: Foundations of Cybersecurity

## Overview

This course is part of the **Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate** on Coursera. It introduces the fundamental principles of cybersecurity and provides a broad understanding of the field, including its importance in modern organizations.

---

## What You’ll Learn

- Core skills required for a cybersecurity analyst  
- How cyber attacks impact business operations  
- Security ethics and professional responsibility  
- Common tools and technologies used in cybersecurity  

---

## Key Concepts

- Introduction to cybersecurity  
- Evolution of cyber threats  
- The eight CISSP security domains  
- Security frameworks and controls  
- The CIA Triad:
  - **Confidentiality**
  - **Integrity**
  - **Availability**

---

## Tools & Technologies

- SIEM tools  
- Introductory exposure to:
  - Linux  
  - SQL  
  - Python  

---

## Outcome

By the end of this course, you will:

- Understand the role of cybersecurity within organizations  
- Recognize common threats and how they impact systems  
- Be prepared to move into more advanced cybersecurity topics  

---

## Real-World Relevance

These concepts form the foundation for:

- Secure system design  
- Risk management strategies  
- Authentication and access control systems  
- Incident detection and response workflows
